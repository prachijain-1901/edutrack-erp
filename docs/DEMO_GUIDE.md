# Screenshot & Demo Guide — EduTrack ERP

Use this guide to capture high-quality assets for your portfolio or investor pitch.

## 📸 Recommended Screenshot List

1.  **The "WOW" Hero**:
    - Route: `/` (Landing Page)
    - Focus: Show the animated dashboard preview and the premium typography.
    - Tip: Use a clean browser frame.

2.  **The Command Center**:
    - Route: `/dashboard`
    - Focus: KPI cards with animated counters and the revenue chart.

3.  **Real-time Attendance**:
    - Route: `/dashboard/attendance/mark`
    - Focus: The clean grid of students and the status toggles.

4.  **WhatsApp Automation**:
    - Route: `/dashboard/whatsapp`

## 💡 Presentation Tips
- **Use Dark Mode**: The app is designed to look premium in dark mode.
- **Show the Mobile View**: EduTrack is fully responsive. Show it on a phone screen to demonstrate accessibility.
- **Highlight the "Real-time" aspect**: Open two windows, mark attendance in one, and show the dashboard stat update in the other.
